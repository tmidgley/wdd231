Hot Air Balloons Site Plan

Site Name

Full of Hot Air

I selected this name because it is memorable, relates directly to hot air balloons, and reflects the fun and exciting nature of ballooning events, rides, and festivals.

Site Purpose

The site provides information about hot air balloons, hot air balloon festivals, and hot air balloon rides. Visitors can learn about upcoming events, watch videos, explore ballooning locations, and sign up for a newsletter to stay informed about future festivals and activities.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find upcoming hot air balloon festivals near me?
  • How much does a hot air balloon ride typically cost?
  • What should I expect during my first hot air balloon ride?
  • What are the most popular hot air balloon festivals in the United States?

Color Scheme

  • #4A90E2 (Sky Blue) - Used for headings, navigation, buttons, and accents.
  • #FF9F1C (Sunrise Orange) - Used for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and important elements.
  • #F8F9FA (Light Gray) - Used for page backgrounds.
  • #222222 (Dark Gray) - Used for body text.

Typography

  • Poppins - Used for headings, section titles, and navigation links.
  • Open Sans - Used for body text, descriptions, and general content.
